Peeching for Freelancing

What is freelancing?


Any work you do for other people, outside your job and doing that gets you paid. Suppose you are a blogger and you know how to install a WordPress blog on a custom domain; You would offer help to other bloggers to host their blog and earn an extra income. Why freelancing? If you are a blogger and: Find it difficult to write a niche content struggle with coming up with great design and User Interface elements for your blog, struggle with maintaining the SEO of the blog You can outsource a skill that you are not good at and the money for the same can be earned by freelancing. Many bloggers started their journey working as a freelancer and trading their skills.
